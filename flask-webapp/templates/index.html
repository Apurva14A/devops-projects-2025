Experience Nature's Grandeur

Nestled amidst the lush green hills of Ramgarh district in Jharkhand, Patratu Valley and Dam offer a breathtaking escape into nature's embrace. Known for its mesmerizing hairpin bends, tranquil waters, and verdant landscapes, it's a paradise for nature lovers, adventure seekers, and those seeking a peaceful retreat from city life.

Key Attractions

Patratu Valley Road (Ghat Road)

The winding, serpentine road leading to the dam is an attraction in itself, often compared to the ghat roads of hill stations. The journey offers panoramic views of the valley, making it a favorite for road trips and photography enthusiasts.

Patratu Dam

A majestic engineering marvel built on the Nalkari River, the dam provides a serene backdrop for relaxation and boating. It's a significant water source and a popular picnic spot, especially during winters when migratory birds flock here.

Patratu Lake & Boating

The vast expanse of the lake at the dam site is perfect for various boating activities. Enjoy a leisurely ride, speed boating, or pedal boating amidst stunning natural beauty.

Nearby Resorts & Parks

Several resorts and well-maintained parks dot the area, offering amenities for families, couples, and groups. These spots often have children's play areas and dining options.

Things To Do

  • Boating: Enjoy a range of boating options from serene pedal boats to thrilling speed boats and even larger cruise boats (charges apply, often ₹250-₹700 depending on boat type and duration).
  • Photography: Capture the stunning landscapes, unique road architecture, and vibrant birdlife.
  • Picnics: With ample green spaces and scenic views, it's an ideal spot for a family picnic.
  • Nature Walks & Trekking: Explore the surrounding dense forests and winding trails for an immersive nature experience.
  • Adventure Activities: Some resorts and adventure parks in the vicinity offer activities like zip-lining, rope courses, and more.
  • Bird Watching: Especially during the winter months (October to March), the dam becomes a haven for various migratory birds.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit

The ideal time to visit Patratu Valley and Dam is from October to March. The weather during these months is cool, pleasant, and perfect for outdoor activities. Monsoons (June to September) offer lush greenery but can make roads slippery, while summers (April-May) can be hot.

Entry Fee & Timings

Valley Road: Free access.
Dam Area: Typically, a nominal entry fee of ₹20-₹50 per person is charged by resort/boat club managing access.
Parking: Approx. ₹50 for cars, ₹20 for bikes.
Boating: Charges vary (e.g., ₹250 for 4-person paddle boat, ₹700 for speed boat).
Timings: Generally open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM for boating and general visitation. It's advised to avoid visiting after dark.

How to Reach

Patratu Valley & Dam is located in the Ramgarh district of Jharkhand, approximately 40 km from Ranchi, the capital city.
By Road: The most popular way to reach is by car or bike from Ranchi, taking about 1 to 1.5 hours. Buses and auto-rickshaws are also available from Ranchi.
Nearest Airport: Birsa Munda Airport (IXR), Ranchi (~42 km).
Nearest Railway Station: Patratu Railway Station (very close), or Ranchi Railway Station (~35 km).

Accommodation

Several resorts and hotels are available in and around Patratu Valley, offering a range of options from budget-friendly to more luxurious stays. It's recommended to book in advance, especially during peak season.
